Zoodles With Pepita Pesto

\

Ingredients

  • 500g. Simply Steam zuuchini spaghetti
  • 1 tbs. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup. pepitas, toasted
  • 1/2 long red chilli, deseeded, finely diced
  • 1/2. bunch basil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 lemon, zested, juiced

Method

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and cook for 1 minute or until just tender. Drain well.
  2. Meanwhile, place oil, pepitas, chilli, basil, garlic and lemon juice in a food processor and process until smooth. Transfer to a large bowl and season with pepper. Add zucchini and 2 tbs water, then toss to coat. Divide among bowls and serve with extra basil and zest.
