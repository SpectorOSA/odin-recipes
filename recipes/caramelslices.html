Chocolate Caramel Slices

Finally, finish off with chocolate caramel slices for dessert. Enjoy this heavenly sweetness with the rest of your love ones.

Ingredients

  • 250g. plain sweet biscuits
  • 380g. can top'n'fill caramel
  • 80g. butter, melted
  • 200g. milk chocolate melts
  • 270g. Woolworths Greek salad kit
  • 1 tbs. vegetable oil

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Crumble biscuits into a food processor. Process until crumbs are fine. Add butter and process until crumbs are wet.
  2. Line a 20x30cm cake tin with baking paper. Press mixture firmly into pan.
  3. Empty caramel into bowl. Whisk until smooth. Pour over biscuit base then tap pan on bench to spread evenly. Bake for 10 minutes then remove from oven. Cool.
  4. Melt chocolate with oil in a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water. Spread chocolate over caramel layer and refrigerate until set. Cut into squares. Note: Melt chocolate over boiling simmer water and keep stirring so it doesn't burn.
