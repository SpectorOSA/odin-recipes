Sausage & Greek Salad Wraps

\

Ingredients

  • 5 ml. olive oil cooking spray
  • 500g. caramelised onion
  • 500g. angus beef sausages
  • 360g. soft wholegrain wraps
  • 270g. Woolworths Greek salad kit
  • 3/4 cup. Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup. continental parsley leaves (to serve)

Method

  1. Spray a chargrill pan with oil and heat over medium-high heat. Cook sausages, turning, for 15 minutes or until cooked through. Transfer to a board and thinly slice diagonally.
  2. Meanwhile, heat wraps according to packet instructions.
  3. Remove tomatoes from salad kit and cut into quarters. Place tomato and remaining salad ingredients in a large bowl and toss to combine.
  4. Place wraps on a clean work surface. Spread each wrap with yoghurt and top with salad and sausage. Scatter with parsley and roll to enclose filling. Serve.
